## The challenge

A neighborhood restaurant wanted to leave "word of mouth" behind without losing its soul. They needed online presence, digital bookings, and a simple way to publish the weekly menu.

## The solution

Static site with **Astro** for maximum speed and strong local SEO. Custom booking system on **Supabase** — no commission-charging platforms. A staff panel to update the menu without touching code.

## Outcome

- Sub-800ms load time on 4G
- Digital bookings reached 60% of total in 2 months
- Zero commissions paid to third-party platforms
