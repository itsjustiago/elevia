## The challenge

The ops team spent 3-4 hours a day consolidating data from 5 different systems into Excel. They asked us for a "single view" — something that showed everything at a glance and let them act on it.

## The solution

**Web app** with real-time sync via Postgres LISTEN/NOTIFY. Direct integration with 5 legacy systems through APIs and controlled scrapers. Role-based permissions so each team sees only what's relevant.

## Outcome

- Manual consolidation hours reduced by **85%**
- Time to identify a delay dropped from 40min to 4min
- 100% internal adoption within 3 weeks
