## The challenge

Local race registration was done on paper. Results were published in PDFs hours later. Participants wanted something better.

## The solution

**iOS and Android** app with sign-up, payment, and live timing. Results visible to the second. Personal history per athlete with social sharing.

## Outcome

- **4.8★** average rating in app stores
- Online registration reached 90% of total
- Deployed at 8 races in the first year
